GeoFS Controls- How To Get Started

ControlActionNotes
+/-Increases/Decreases thrustHolding the - for some time will activate reverse thrust
ETurns on/off enginesIf you bounce too hard or make an extremely hard landing,

the engine may shut off and will not respawn unless you hit

'R' or reload the page.

RRestartSpawns you back on your starting location. Due to its proximity to T, it is frequently pressed at the worst times.
[ ]Flaps/Slats/Droops[ = extend ] = retract
BSpoilers/air brakesSlows your aircraft down
GGearRetracts/extends gear
XAnimateIf your plane has an animation (ex. opening cargo nose door),

X will perform it. Not all airplanes have animation.

CCameraToggles through camera views
Down ArrowElevator UpLifts nose up
Up arrowElevator downDescends
Left ArrowAileron LeftTurns left WHEN IN AIR and when Mix Roll/Yaw enabled.

If you are taking off with, crosswind, push the arrow in which

the wind is coming from (eg Winds coming from left, press

left arrow) to prevent banking during takeoff

Right ArrowAileron RightTurns right WHEN IN AIR and when Mix Roll/Yaw enabled.

If you are taking off with, crosswind, push the arrow in which

the wind is coming from (eg Winds coming from left, press

left arrow) to prevent banking during takeoff

,Rudder LeftMoves your aircraft left, no matter if in the air or on ground (works only if mix roll/yaw is disabled)
.Rudder RightMoves your aircraft right, no matter if in the air or on ground (works only if mix roll/yaw is disabled)
0,1,2,3..7,8,9Thrust settings0 = 10% thrust, 1 = 20% thrust, 2 = 30% thrust, etc

Note that 10% thrust is IDLE and E turns engines off completely

PPauses gameIf you see a fire in your house, please do consider pausing.
AAutopilotBe sure to change the autopilot settings in Nav
TTalk/ChatIf you type something and hit enter, you will be chatting. BE CAREFUL: two !'s, ?'s and other weird characters will clog up your chat and you will have to reload. forward slash doesn't work so use the backwards slash \
Shift/SpaceBrakesHit this when you are landing/need to slow down. It is also recommended you dab the brakes after you rotate.
Semi Colon (;)PARKING BrakeHit this ONLY if you will be holding/stopping for a long time

for lineup, pre-flight checks, etc

SSoundThe sound is VERY loud on GeoFS. You may consider

setting your sound to 0 and gradually increasing it.
